About Us

A United Team

Our team consits of around 50 devoted, hardworking members.

Experience Science Like Never Before

Our team competes in 23 events in groups of 2-3 students. Events cover specific, specialized topics in fields such as chemistry, biology, physics, engineering, and inquiry/nature of science. Students can do anything from building airplanes to taking a test on different types of fossils. Science Olympiad prepares students to become active members of the scientific community, equipping them to become the leaders of tommorow.

Compete with us!

Each year, we travel to many exciting new places for competitions. Join us on our journey to Princeton, Yale, UPenn, and so much more.
